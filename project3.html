Experimental Clock - Nap Alarm

Project Description

With a busy schedule, it is often difficult to get enough sleep. Sleep can being to feel like a chore and inefficient use of time. To promote a quick recharge during the day, I have created a playful restfulness meter for a quick 60 minute nap.

This project explores time units in P5, and visualizing time passed in real time
