Exquisite Corpse (Collaborative Project)

Project Description

This robo-spirit-squid frankenstein is born out of a mad scientist's lab. They wanted to invent something that is wired with a set of rules and instructions, yet invisible and lightweight, while being able to navigate in deep waters. They are a new form of data weaponry taking in information from the darkest corners of this earth.

This project explores collaboration through a surrealist technique where each member draws a section of the body, which is combined to form a new character narrative
