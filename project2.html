Face Generator

Project Description

In my own practice, I tend to lean on colors to give my work meaning and depth, and I wanted to translate that style to a P5 project. The random colors of the eyes aim to contrast the round and smooth appearance of the face to evoke a frantic feeling of being put on the spot paird with a pink color resemling blushing under pressure.
